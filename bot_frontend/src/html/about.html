What is WednesdayOS Bot?

WednesdayOS Bot is an Instagram bot that can perform operations that you usually can't do or maybe hard for you.

Such as:

- Checking who unfollowed you since you registered.

- Cool stats about your account.


Our instagram bot account needs to be following your account for it to work.

Once you're registered you will receive a follow from our bot, please accept it.

The bot was developed in January 2023, but recently I decided to publish it as a WebApp.

So, it's still in the process of being ported.
